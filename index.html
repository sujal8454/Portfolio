My Story

"I didn't wait for the perfect moment — I built the skills, launched the channel, and started freelancing while still in college."

While most students were focused purely on academics, Sujal was building something bigger. Combining his B.Com education with hands-on creative work, he launched a YouTube channel called Gadget Chaos, started freelancing on Upwork, built a presence on LinkedIn, and mastered the tools that matter in today's digital economy.

Every video edited, every ad campaign launched, every client project delivered was a lesson. The result: a multi-disciplinary creative professional who understands not just how to make content look good — but how to make it work.
